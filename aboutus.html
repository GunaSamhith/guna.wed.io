ABOUT ANIME

Anime is a Japanese form of animation that's gained global popularity, distinguished by its unique art style, often featuring vibrant colors, large expressive eyes, and dynamic characters. The term "anime" can refer to any animated work in Japan, but outside of Japan, it specifically denotes Japanese animation.
